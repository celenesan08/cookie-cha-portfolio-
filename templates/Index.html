🍪 About Us

At Cookiecha, we bake happiness one cookie at a time! Our boba drinks & cookies are made fresh daily using premium ingredients, inspired by a blend of classic flavors and Asian desserts. Whether you’re craving a sweet treat or a refreshing drink, we’ve got something to brighten your day.

Founded in 2025, Cookiecha started as a small kitchen experiment and grew into a beloved spot for cookie lovers across Sweet City. Thank you for supporting local bakers!
